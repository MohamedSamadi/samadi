First Lego League (FLL)

The First Lego League (FLL) is a global robotics competition that combines creativity, engineering, and teamwork. Participants design, build, and program robots to solve complex challenges. I actively participate in FLL, using my passion for robotics to innovate and learn.

  • Core Values: Innovation, teamwork, and inclusion.
  • Robotic Challenges: Programming robots to complete missions on a themed playing field.
  • Research Projects: Exploring real-world problems and presenting innovative solutions.
